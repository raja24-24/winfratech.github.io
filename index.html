		India produces one of the highest number of structural engineers in the world, but their employability has been a concern at both national and international levels. 
		While there exists a gap between what is learned in the class and what is expected at work, bridging this ‘Skill Gap’ has been a challenge of its own. 
		TPSE aims to identify and fill this gap through innovatively designed training programs for both students and working professionals
